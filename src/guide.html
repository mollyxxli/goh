Game of Health , aka GOT.


is a Rouguelike game website for teengaers to have better understanding of contemporary health issues

4 Attibutes

  • Health - Your health condition through the game

  • Wealth - Your money usage on different cases

  • Pressure - sress you are taken at different stages

  • Social-Reliation - Relationship with other people

    • Ground Rules

    1. The changing values on attributes are determined by the law of addiction and subtraction.

    2. The value change range is 0 to 2     3. When the value turns red, it is warning you that you are close to fail the game

    4. If Social-relation or Pressure reach 10, you fail the game

    5. If Health or Wealth reach the maximum value, there is no negative side effect and it will stop incrementing

    6. Users are able to turn on/off the background music

    Game Scenarios

    Users will experience a variety of interesting stories, ranging from topics on Sunburn, Alcohole Abuse, Season Flu, Junk Food, Smoking, Mental Health
